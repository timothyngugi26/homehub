About HomeHub

What is HomeHub?

HomeHub is an innovative after-school program that brings technology education, mentorship, and faith-based learning directly to residential communities. We partner with real estate developers and property managers to establish dedicated learning spaces within residential estates.

Our Mission

To empower the next generation with technological skills, mentorship, and spiritual guidance in accessible community spaces.

Our Vision

A world where every child has access to quality tech education and mentorship within their living community, fostering both intellectual and spiritual growth.
